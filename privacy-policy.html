Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the SSH ADB Telnet Remote app (hereby referred to as "Application") for mobile devices that was created by Philipp Thiele (hereby referred to as "Service Provider") as an Ad Supported service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

Remote Credentials and Terminal Data

The Application facilitates remote connections via SSH, ADB, and Telnet. All sensitive credentials, including SSH private keys, passwords, and terminal session data, are stored exclusively on your device. The Service Provider does not have access to, nor does it transmit, your server credentials or terminal history to any external servers.

Information Collection and Use

The Application collects information through third-party SDKs when you download and use it. This information may include:

  • Your device's Internet Protocol address (IP address)
  • Operating system version and device identifiers
  • Approximate Location: The Application utilizes location data via AdMob to provide personalized content and relevant advertisements.

Third Party Access

Only aggregated, anonymized data is transmitted to external services to aid the Service Provider in improving the Application. The Application utilizes third-party services that have their own Privacy Policies:

  • Google Play Services
  • AdMob
